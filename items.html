Xbox 360

The Xbox 360, released in 2005 as Microsoft's second console, was a defining seventh-generation platform that popularized high-definition gaming and robust online multiplayer via Xbox Live. It thrived with popular exclusives, a user-friendly dashboard, and significant hardware revisions, though it was famously plagued by the "Red Ring of Death" failure.
